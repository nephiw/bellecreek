Covenant Enforcement Meetings

Belle Creek does not have an HOA, we have the Covenant Enforcement Committee (CEC) which has a similar role. So what is the difference? An entire HOA is organized around covenants; definition, enforcement, communication, etc. Instead, we have a Metropolitian District with a single committee for covenants, but also does much more. Our Covenant Enforcement Committee helps with definitions and communication, but helping neighbors is the real mission. Once a month, the CEC meets with a represenative of the Enforcment Management Company (Wolfersberger LLC) and the public to discuss enforcement issues, to work with home owners to get on the same page. This committee has to find ways of being fair but working with the problems that life throws at us.

The CEC meetings are held once a month on the third Wednesday. These meetings are open to the public and we encourage the home owners join us if they want to hear what is going on or express some other concern.
